EulerAngles

public struct EulerAngles

EulerAngles

Discussion:

Type represents a Euler angles (one way of parameterizing rotation). The Euler angles are three angles describe the orientation of a rigid body. To describe such an orientation in 3-dimensional Euclidean space three parameters are required:

    [roll, pich, yaw]
