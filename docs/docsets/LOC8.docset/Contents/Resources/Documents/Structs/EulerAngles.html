EulerAngles

public struct EulerAngles: CustomStringConvertible

Type represents a Euler angles (one way of parameterizing rotation).

Discussion:

The Euler angles are three angles describe the orientation of a rigid body. To describe such an orientation in 3-dimensional Euclidean space three parameters are required: [roll, pich, yaw]
